Frequently Asked Questions

Our admission process is designed to identify students who will thrive in our academic environment. We look for a strong academic record, extracurricular involvement, and a passion for learning. Specific requirements vary by grade level.

Our fee structure is comprehensive and covers tuition, standard materials, and access to campus facilities. For a detailed breakdown of fees for the upcoming academic year, please download our fee schedule from the admissions page or contact the finance office.

Yes, we offer a range of merit-based and need-based scholarships to deserving students. Our goal is to make quality education accessible. Information on scholarship criteria and the application process can be found on our website.

We believe in holistic development. Our school offers a wide array of extracurricular activities, including sports (soccer, basketball, swimming), arts (drama, music, painting), and clubs (debate, science, coding). A full list is available in our student handbook.

We maintain a low student-teacher ratio of approximately 15:1 to ensure personalized attention and effective learning for every student. This allows our teachers to cater to individual student needs and foster a supportive classroom environment.
